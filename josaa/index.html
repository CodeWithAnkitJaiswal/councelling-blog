    WHAT IS JoSAA???

    Through JoSAA(Joint Sear Allocation Authority) you can take admission for B.Tech/B.Arch to many institutes which includes IITs,NITs,IIITs,GFTIs(Government Funded Technical Institutes).
    Those candidates who qualify JEE Mains or JEE Advanced must register for JoSAA to participate in the counselling and get their dream college.

    Total Institutes In JoSAA Counselling Are-

    • 23 IIts (Indian Institute of Technology)
    • 31 NITs (National Institute of Technology)
    • 26 IIITs (Indian Institute of Information Technology)
    • 38 GFTIs (Government Funded Technical Institutes)

    HOW TO APPLY FOR JoSAA???

    Step-1>

    Firstly, Candidates have to REGISTER for counselling at JoSAA Portal using their JEE Main or JEE Advanced roll number and password.

    Step-2>

    After registration, candidates need to fill in their preferred choices of institutes and courses in order of preference which is know as CHOICE FILLING. It's important to carefully research and prioritize choices based on personal preferences, rank, and availability of seats.

    Step-3>

    Once the choices are filled, candidates must LOCK THEIR CHOICES within the specified time frame. After locking, no changes can be made to the choices.

    Step-4>

    Based on the candidate's JEE rank, choices filled, category, and availability of seats, the JoSAA conducts multiple rounds of seat allocation. SEAT ALLOCATION results are published on the JoSAA Portal.

    Step-5>

    Candidates who are allocated seats need to INDICATE THEIR ACCEPTANCE by choosing one of the following options:

    Freeze:

    Accept the allocated seat and choose not to participate in further rounds.

    Float:

    Accept the allocated seat but also opt to be considered for other institutes of higher preference in subsequent rounds.

    Slide:

    Accept the allocated seat but choose a different course in the same institute during subsequent rounds.

    Withdraw:

    Choose to withdraw from the seat allocation process altogether.

    Step-6>

    After seat acceptance, candidates need to report to the designated reporting centers for DOCUMENT VERIFICATION AN CONFIRMATION OF ADMISSION. Documents usually include:

    VACANT SEATS AFTER JoSAA COUNSELLING.


    The Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) conducts 6 rounds of seat allocation to facilitate admissions to various Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs), National Institutes of Technology (NITs), Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs), and other Government Funded Technical Institutes (GFTIs).
