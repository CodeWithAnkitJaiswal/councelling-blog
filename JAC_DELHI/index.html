    WHAT IS JAC Delhi???

    The Joint Admission Committee (JAC) Delhi streamlines the admission process for various undergraduate programs by conducting a centralized counselling session. This eliminates the need for students to participate in individual college admissions, saving them time and effort.

    HOW TO APPLY FOR JAC Delhi???

    Step-1>

    Registration: Prospective students must register on the JAC Delhi website (https://jacdelhi.admissions.nic.in/) using their JEE Main rank and other required credentials.

    Step-2>

    Choice Filling: This crucial step involves prioritizing your preferred colleges and programs based on your interests and JEE Main rank. Strategic choice filling can significantly improve your chances of securing a desired seat.

    Step-3>

    Locking Choices: Once you've finalized your preferences, ensure you lock your choices to confirm them.

    Step-4>

    Seat Allotment: JAC Delhi will conduct multiple rounds of seat allotment. Regularly check the website to view your allotted seat based on your rank and choices.

    Step-5>

    Acceptance and Fee Payment: If you accept the allotted seat, promptly pay the admission fee at the designated institute to confirm your place.

    Step-5>

    Upgradation (Optional): If you're not satisfied with the initial allotment, you can opt for seat upgradation in subsequent rounds (availability subject to confirmation).

    Remember to stay updated with any notifications or announcements from JAC Delhi regarding the admission process and important dates. It's essential to adhere to deadlines and provide accurate information throughout the application process.

    IMPORTANT POINTS TO REMEMBER.


  • 1. Document Verification: Keep all the required documents ready for upload/verification, such as JEE Main Admit Card, Score Card, Class 10th and 12th Marksheets, Category Certificate (if applicable), and a recent photograph.

  • 2. Personal Details: Double-check your personal details like name, date of birth, category, etc., as entered during JEE Main registration, as these will be carried forward to the JAC Delhi registration.

  • 3. Choice Filling: Carefully go through the list of institutes and courses offered, and plan your preferences wisely. You can alter your choices later, but it's better to have a well-thought-out initial preference list.

  • 4. Password Creation: Create a strong and unique password for your JAC Delhiaccount, and keep it safe for future use during the counseling process.

  • 5. Email and Mobile Number: Provide a valid and accessible email address and mobile number, as important communications fromJAC Delhi will be sent via these channels.

  • 6. Fee Payment: Have the necessary fee payment methods (online/offline) ready, as you may need to pay theJAC Delhi registration fee during the process.

  • 7. Deadlines: Strictly adhere to the deadlines set by JAC Delhifor registration, choice filling, and other activities. Missing a deadline can lead to the cancellation of your candidature.

  • 8. Authenticity: Only use the officialJAC Delhi website (https://jacdelhi.admissions.nic.in/) for registration and counseling purposes. Beware of any unofficial or fraudulent websites.

  • 9. Seek Assistance: If you face any issues or have doubts, reach out to theJAC Delhi helpline or seek assistance from your school/college authorities.
