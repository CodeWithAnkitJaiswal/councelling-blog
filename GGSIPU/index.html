    WHAT IS GGISPU???

    Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), located in India, conducts counseling sessions for various academic programs. Here are some details about its counseling process:

    GGSIPU conducts online counseling sessions for admission to various programs.

    For B.Tech programs (Code: 131), candidates must apply separately for the IPU CET (Common Entrance Test) and submit their JEE Main details.

    To participate in the counseling, candidates must also apply for GGSIPU counseling.

    HOW TO APPLY FOR GGISPU???

    Step-1>

    Firstly, Candidates have to REGISTER for counselling at GGISPU Portal. You will likely need to provide basic details such as your name, date of birth, email address, mobile number, etc.

    Step-2>

    After registration, you’ll need to fill in your preferred choices of colleges and courses. Lock your choices to finalize them. The whole process is known as CHOICE FILLING AND LOCKING.

    Step-3>

    Based on your choices and merit, seats will be allocated to candidates. You’ll receive an allotment letter indicating your college and course. This is known as SEAT ALLOTMENT.

    Step-4>

    Attend the DOCUMENT VERIFICATION process as per the schedule. Ensure you have all the necessary documents ready.

    TO KNOW ABOUT DOCUMENT DETAILED INFORMATION, YOU CAN CHECK THIS PAGE BY CLICK HERE.

    Step-5>

    Once you receive the seat allotment, REPORT TO THE ALLOTED COLLEGE within the specified time frame. Complete the admission formalities and start your academic journey at GGSIPU.

    Remember to stay updated with any notifications or announcements from GGISPU regarding the admission process and important dates. It's essential to adhere to deadlines and provide accurate information throughout the application process.

    IMPORTANT POINTS TO REMEMBER.


  • 1. Document Verification: Keep all the required documents ready for upload/verification, such as JEE Main Admit Card, Score Card, Class 10th and 12th Marksheets, Category Certificate (if applicable), and a recent photograph.

  • 2. Personal Details: Double-check your personal details like name, date of birth, category, etc., as entered during JEE Main registration, as these will be carried forward to the GGISPU registration.

  • 3. Choice Filling: Carefully go through the list of institutes and courses offered, and plan your preferences wisely. You can alter your choices later, but it's better to have a well-thought-out initial preference list.

  • 4. Password Creation: Create a strong and unique password for your GGISPU account, and keep it safe for future use during the counseling process.

  • 5. Email and Mobile Number: Provide a valid and accessible email address and mobile number, as important communications from GGISPU will be sent via these channels.

  • 6. Fee Payment: Have the necessary fee payment methods (online/offline) ready, as you may need to pay the GGISPU registration fee during the process.

  • 7. Deadlines: Strictly adhere to the deadlines set by GGISPU for registration, choice filling, and other activities. Missing a deadline can lead to the cancellation of your candidature.

  • 8. Authenticity: Only use the official GGISPU website (http://www.ipu.ac.in/) for registration and counseling purposes. Beware of any unofficial or fraudulent websites.

  • 9. Seek Assistance: If you face any issues or have doubts, reach out to the GGISPU helpline or seek assistance from your school/college authorities.
