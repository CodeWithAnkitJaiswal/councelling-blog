    WHAT IS MHTCET???

    MHT CET stands for Maharashtra Common Entrance Test. It's an entrance exam conducted by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Government of Maharashtra. MHT CET is the gateway to various undergraduate professional courses offered by universities and colleges in Maharashtra.

    Eligibility Criteria


    Nationality: Indian nationals Educational.

    Qualification: Passed 10+2 or equivalent exam from a recognized board/university with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.

    Minimum Marks:
    General Category: 50% aggregate in 12th standard exam.
    SC/ST/OBC Category: 45% aggregate in 12th standard exam.

    No Age Limit: There is no upper age limit for appearing in MHT CET

    HOW TO APPLY FOR MHTCET???

    Step-1>

    Registration & Fee Payment: After the MHT CET results, register online on the official website (cetcell.mahacet.org) and pay the counselling fee.

    Step-2>

    Document Verification: Upload scanned copies of required documents like MHT CET admit card, mark sheet, caste certificate (if applicable), etc., for online verification.

    Step-3>

    Choice Filling: Fill out your preferred colleges and courses based on your rank and seat availability. You can prioritize your choices for better allotment chances.

    Step-4>

    Seat Allotment: The counselling authority releases seat allotment lists based on candidate rank, preferences, and seat availability.

    Step-5>

    Confirmation & Reporting: If allotted a seat, confirm your acceptance by paying the seat confirmation fee. Then, report to the allotted college for completing the admission process.

    Remember to stay updated with any notifications or announcements from MHTCET regarding the admission process and important dates. It's essential to adhere to deadlines and provide accurate information throughout the application process.

    IMPORTANT POINTS TO REMEMBER.


  • 1. Document Verification: Keep all the required documents ready for upload/verification, such as JEE Main Admit Card, Score Card, Class 10th and 12th Marksheets, Category Certificate (if applicable), and a recent photograph.

  • 2. Personal Details: Double-check your personal details like name, date of birth, category, etc., as entered during JEE Main registration, as these will be carried forward to the MHTCET registration.

  • 3. Choice Filling: Carefully go through the list of institutes and courses offered, and plan your preferences wisely. You can alter your choices later, but it's better to have a well-thought-out initial preference list.

  • 4. Password Creation: Create a strong and unique password for your MHTCET account, and keep it safe for future use during the counseling process.

  • 5. Email and Mobile Number: Provide a valid and accessible email address and mobile number, as important communications from MHTCET will be sent via these channels.

  • 6. Fee Payment: Have the necessary fee payment methods (online/offline) ready, as you may need to pay the MHTCET registration fee during the process.

  • 7. Deadlines: Strictly adhere to the deadlines set by MHTCET for registration, choice filling, and other activities. Missing a deadline can lead to the cancellation of your candidature.

  • 8. Authenticity: Only use the official MHTCET website (https://cetcell.mahacet.org/) for registration and counseling purposes. Beware of any unofficial or fraudulent websites.

  • 9. Seek Assistance: If you face any issues or have doubts, reach out to the MHTCET helpline or seek assistance from your school/college authorities.

  • 10. You can participate in the counselling only if you qualify in the MHT CET exam.
